## DESIGN GUIDELINES

1. **Clean and professional** - Not flashy or gimmicky
2. **Plenty of white space** - Don't crowd sections
3. **Alternate section backgrounds** - White, then #f8fafc, then white
4. **Mobile responsive** - Must look great on phones
5. **Smooth scroll** - Clicking nav links scrolls smoothly to sections
6. **Subtle animations** - Fade in on scroll, hover effects on buttons
7. **No stock photos of fake people** - Use icons, illustrations, or abstract graphics instead
